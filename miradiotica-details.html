Mi Radio Tica - iOS App

Project information

  • Technologies: SwiftUI, API Networking, Model View View Model (MVVM), AVFoundation, Media Player, Combine, StoreKit
  • Year of Release: 2022
  • Website
  • App Store URL

Mi Radio Tica is an iOS App for iPhone and iPad devices that provides users the option to enjoy listening to the best radio stations of Costa Rica, with a clean and modern interface.

Features:

  • - Listen to radio stations of Costa Rica
  • - Add radio stations to your list of favorites
  • - Keep listening when the application is running in the background
  • - Use Airplay to stream the audio to compatible devices
